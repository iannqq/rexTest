Constellation Creation | 1/55

Create a star nearby the player, following you. Once 5 stars are created, they form a constellation, mining all blocks between them.

Asteroid Rush | 1/78

10 to 25 tiny asteroids are shot out in a 180 degree spread in front of you, mining ores in a tiny radius around them upon impact.

Meteor Strike | 1/145

Two to five meteors rain down from the sky, exploding upon impact to mine ores in a small radius.

Comet Dash | 1/215

Become frozen inside a comet, shooting yourself forwards at a high speed and mining all blocks in your path. The comet's speed scales with your walkspeed.

Lustrous Ribbon

Increases proc rate by +5%, health by +75, sets jump to 8, and sets speed to 27.

Ornament Bomb | 1/10 | 1.4x Luck

Shoot an ornament in a random direction, mining blocks in a radius of 1.

Bell Toll | 1/65 | 2x Luck

A bell above your head rings, generating a sonic pulse that mines blocks in a ring-shaped area in a radius of 4.

Tangle of Lights | 1/55 | 3x Luck

Christmas Gifts | 1/300 | Varied Luck

A gift is given to every member in the server, having varying effects based on the color of the gift. Gifts can be given only every minute, with the exception of a red gift.

Red Gift | 5x Luck

Red gifts burst open with 4-9 toys, each bursting to mine blocks in a 2 block radius.

Blue Gift | 3x Luck

Blue gifts open up and suck all non-layer ores in a radius of 37.5 blocks.

Green Gift | 3x Luck

Green gifts open up and grant the player temporary luck bonus of 3 to all abilities. This gift overrides other luck boost - doesn't stack.

Yellow Gift

Yellow gifts open up to boost the player's pickaxe ability rates by 50%. This gift stacks with other ability boosts.
